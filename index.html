CIPTA 2027

A platform for collaboration-driven innovation with regional reach.

The current edition emphasizes connections between academia, industry, government, society, and technology so bold ideas can grow into practical and lasting impact.

Why This Matters

Official 2027 details are now front and center

The page now reflects the live competition cycle, including the new theme, current launch date, and submission milestones through 2027.

Audience Focus

Designed for inventors, schools, and institutions

The official scope covers schools, technical and tertiary institutions, public and private organisations, start-ups, entrepreneurs, and the general public.

Competition Scope

Science, engineering, technology, and innovation

CIPTA continues to position itself as a biennial platform for showcasing inventions and technological advancement from Brunei Darussalam and the ASEAN region.

Patron

Royal patronage remains central to the award’s identity.

The competition is graced with the patronage of the Crown Prince and Senior Minister at the Prime Minister’s Office, His Royal Highness Prince (Dr.) Haji Al-Muhtadee Billah, with the consent of His Majesty the Sultan and Yang Di-Pertuan of Brunei Darussalam.

This patronage gives CIPTA its prestige and underpins the Crown Prince’s Creative, Innovative Product and Technological Advancement Special Award presented to the overall winner.

Theme

Bridge. Innovate. Beyond

In an increasingly interconnected world, CIPTA 2027 frames innovation as something that thrives when ideas, people, disciplines, and technologies are meaningfully connected.

The theme calls participants to bridge knowledge, industries, cultures, and technologies so solutions can move beyond conventional boundaries and create lasting impact.

Bridge

Connects academia, industry, government, and society through shared innovation.

Innovate

Encourages emerging technologies, multidisciplinary thinking, and practical solutions.

Beyond

Challenges participants to push past existing frameworks and scale bold ideas further.

Competition Categories

Three pathways, tailored to different innovators.

Local Categories

Open to Brunei Darussalam participants across public and institutional tracks

  • Creating new products, processes, or technologies
  • Modifying existing products, processes, or technologies
  • Includes young inventor participation in upper secondary years

Smart Junior

Created as an earlier entry point for lower secondary school students

  • Targets Year 7 to Year 9 or equivalent
  • Builds innovation culture earlier in the education pipeline
  • Supports participation from schools and younger inventors

ASEAN Categories

Invites inventors and youth participants from ASEAN member states

  • New invention and innovation category
  • Young inventor category for ages 15 to 18
  • Strengthens regional participation and collaboration

About CIPTA

A biennial platform for invention, innovation, and regional recognition.

Patronage and prestige

The competition is held under the patronage of His Royal Highness Prince (Dr.) Haji Al-Muhtadee Billah and carries the prestige of the Crown Prince’s CIPTA Special Award.

Scope and participation

Held biennially since 2005, CIPTA covers science, engineering, technology, and innovation, with participation open to schools, institutions, organisations, start-ups, entrepreneurs, and the public.

Objectives

The official objectives emphasize inspiring youth, encouraging invention, recognising talent, stimulating research and development, promoting commercialisation, and strengthening collaboration within Brunei Darussalam and ASEAN.
